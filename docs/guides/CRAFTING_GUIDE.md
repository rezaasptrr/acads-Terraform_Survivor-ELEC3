## ğŸ¯ How to Craft

1. **Gather Resources**

   - Press **E** to explore for wood, stone, berries
   - Press **H** to hunt for meat

2. **Open Crafting Menu**

   - Press **C** or click "Craft" button

3. **Check Requirements**

   - Green text = You have enough
   - Red text = Need more resources

4. **Craft Item**
   - Click "Craft" button on available recipes
   - Item is crafted and effect applied instantly!

## ğŸ“ Advanced Tips

### Resource Priority

1. **Wood** - Most versatile, needed for everything
2. **Stone** - Important for major crafts
3. **Berries** - Dual purpose (eat or craft)
4. **Meat** - Better to eat than use for crafting

### Crafting vs. Actions

**Craft Spear vs. Hunt:**

- Spear: Safe, costs 3 wood + 2 stone, get 3-8 meat
- Hunt: Risky, costs 20 energy, might fail and lose 15 HP

**Craft Bandage vs. Eat Food:**

- Bandage: +25 HP, costs 2 wood + 3 berries
- Berries: +15 Hunger (prevents HP loss)
- Choose based on immediate need!

### Emergency Crafting

**Critical Health (< 20 HP):**

1. Craft Bandage (+25 HP) - Fastest
2. Craft Shelter (+15 HP, +30 Energy) - Best overall
3. Craft Campfire (+10 HP, +15 Energy) - Cheaper

**Low Energy (< 20):**

1. Rest (free, +40 energy)
2. Craft Shelter (+30 energy, +15 HP)
3. Craft Campfire (+15 energy, +10 HP)
