**"I clicked but nothing happened"**

- Only berries and meat are edible
- Wood and stone can't be eaten!

**"I don't have any food"**

- Press **H** to hunt for meat (risky!)
- Check your inventory to see what you have

**"My hunger is still low"**

- Eat multiple times if needed
- Each berry restores 15 hunger
- Each meat restores 30 hunger
- Max hunger is 100
